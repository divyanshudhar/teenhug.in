Presents - T - Flare

Introduction

T-flare a Smart wearable-helmet is a business model which throws attention on every rider's journey who roust to commute between different destinations.

Description

TeenHug is a future logistic module which focuses on every individuals expectations from the present tech-era and caters to every demand of the future generations.

Our Product

T-Flare helmets are mainly based on smart journey by linking phone connections with convenience and maintaining decorum of safety at the same time.

Features

Extended Battery Life

10-40 hours of an uninterrupted smart helmet experience awaits you. As we develop further, our OTA upgrades will make your application more and more efficient as well, to better battery life. Join us on the road!

Premium Unispeaker Audio

We know how much of a difference great audio can make, so we worked on it. Enjoy seamless echo cancellation and noise reduction, all while concentrating audio to a single side so as to not disrupt your road awareness .

Fast Bluetooth Connectivity

No messy connections. Simply pair your helmet once, and watch it connect automatically with your phone through power-saving BLE technology as soon as you switch it on! What's more, regular over-the-air upgrades make your experience an ever evolving one .

Easy Audio Navigation

Never look away from the road to check directions again. Get turn by turn, two-wheeler specific voice navigation carried to you directly, all within your helmet. Ride along and never miss a right turn!

Gesture/Touch Enabled Functions

No more looking down at mobile mounts, or squeezing your phone under your helmet when riding. Accept and reject calls, get notifications and send messages, all from inside the helmet itself, with a single swipe. Trigger Google Assistant effortlessly, and be on your way.

Enhanced Safety Mode and Helmet Wear Detection

The T-Flare Smart subsystems automatically detect whether or not you're wearing the helmet, and will only activate functionalities on proper wear. Get ready to stay safer on the road, all while having a better ride!

Emergency sos

Which gets activated in any case of any emergency with just A single click you can send an emergency alert and send your location to your contacts in seconds.

Accident detection and alerts

Our accident detection technology lets the helmet detect any accidents or impacts you may have, and immediately relay the location and time of the incident to your emergency contacts. We intend to ensure that safety is never compromised on.
